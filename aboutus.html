About

Beto Frías | Andrés M. Campuzano

   


ARQUIDROMO was founded in 2011 by Mexican architects Beto Frías and Andrés M. Campuzano. The firm has worked in the architectural conceptualization and design of residential, commercial, tourism and cultural projects around Mexico, South America and the United States.

The organic, atypical and complex gestures of their architecture are the result of a rigorous research process; a formal and spatial exploration exercise powered by parametric design software; and a fascination for challenging and optimizing traditional typologies. This approach produces bold buildings that benefit from and respond to their urban, environmental, economic and cultural context in order to enrich the experience offered to their inhabitants.
